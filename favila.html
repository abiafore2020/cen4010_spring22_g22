I am a computer science major and I am currently in my junior year. I have attended FAU since Fall 2021. Additionally, I was born in Cuba, but I moved to Florida in 2014 when I was eleven years old and it has been my home ever since. I got introduced to coding and a very little overview of the computer science world by some of my friends in high school who were in the computer science academy and since then it became what I wanted to study in college. I am still not sure on what area of CS I want to focus on, but I would love it to be on something creative where I could mix coding with graphic design.
